New paper out in Cell Reports by Yvonne Chen et al.!

In this manuscript, Yvonne Chen and colleagues characterize hippocampal ripples across a number of cognitive states (rest, sleep, task…). Really cool work looking at ‘what’ the spectral features of true-ripples may be, how to identify them, and how they may or may not vary across states. Check it out here!
